Research Internship

Adaptive Multi-Modal Fusion Transformer for Indian Sign Language Recognition

 National Institute of Technology, Calicut  |   May 14, 2026 — July 2026 (2 Months)

Developed a deep learning-based Indian Sign Language (ISL) recognition system using RGB video frames and pose landmarks. The project integrates ResNet50, Transformer networks, and multimodal fusion techniques to classify sign gestures. Various fusion strategies were evaluated — RGB Baseline, Late Fusion, and Adaptive Fusion — with the best models achieving 81.82% test accuracy. The system incorporates explainable AI via Grad-CAM and attention map analysis to improve interpretability.
